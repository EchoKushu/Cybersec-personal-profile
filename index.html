About Echo Kushu

At Echo Kushu, we specialise in delivering cutting-edge cybersecurity, data protection, and compliance solutions designed to safeguard modern businesses. Our mission is simple: to help organisations build resilience against evolving cyber threats while ensuring they remain compliant with international standards and regulations.

With a portfolio spanning penetration testing, red teaming, data protection, compliance consulting, and managed security services, we provide end-to-end coverage — from identifying vulnerabilities to implementing proactive defence strategies.

Our team is built on expertise, trust, and innovation. By aligning with frameworks such as CREST, ISO, GDPR, SOC 2, and DORA, we bring clients the assurance that their digital assets, systems, and people are protected to the highest standard.

Whether it’s securing a growing startup, strengthening an enterprise, or guiding organisations through complex compliance landscapes, Echo Kushu is your trusted partner for security, resilience, and growth in the digital era.
